# Day 05 - If Else Statements

## What I Studied

- if statement
- else statement
- Comparison operators

## What I Learned

The if statement allows a program to make decisions.

The else statement executes when the condition in the if statement is false.

## Comparison Operators

- == Equal To
- != Not Equal To
- > Greater Than
- < Less Than
- > = Greater Than or Equal To
- <= Less Than or Equal To

## Example

Age = 22

Condition:

age >= 18

Result:

Eligible to vote

## Real Life Usage

- Login systems
- ATM machines
- Voting eligibility
- Grade calculations
- Temperature checks

## Key Takeaway

If-Else statements are the foundation of decision making in programming.
